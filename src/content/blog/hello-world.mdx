## Why Astro?

Astro is the perfect choice for a content-heavy site like a portfolio.

1. **Zero JS by default**: Interactive islands only where needed.
2. **Content Collections**: Type-safe markdown/MDX.
3. **View Transitions**: Built-in SPA-like navigation.

## The Stack

- **Astro** for the framework
- **Tailwind CSS** (v4!) for styling
- **React** for interactive components (like the sandbox)
